## Description

Cypher is an established hero whose work rarely makes the big headlines —
methodical, precise, operating in the spaces other heroes overlook. John
discovered him through news coverage and became quietly obsessed long
before Cypher ever noticed him. His real name is unknown to Vigil, and
he has not offered it.

## Role

Primary mentor to [[Wiki/PCs/Vigil]]. Embodies Danger; denies Superior.
Has provided the team with his hidden base, supercomputer, and surveillance
equipment. Former mentor to [[Wiki/NPCs/Meridian]], whose relationship
with him is complicated.

## Party Relationship

Vigil holds Cypher in near-reverence. Cypher recruited him deliberately
and asked him specifically to keep an eye on [[Wiki/PCs/Glitch]] —
reasons unstated. Holds permanent Influence over Vigil by Protégé rule.

## Influence

Holds permanent Influence over [[Wiki/PCs/Vigil]] by the Protégé mechanic.
This cannot be removed except through advancement.

## What the Team Knows

Established hero, methodical and effective. Embodies decisive, dangerous
action. Skeptical of Superior-style cleverness. Has resources. Former
mentor to Meridian. Real name unknown.

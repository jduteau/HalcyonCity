## Description

Meridian went through what Vigil is going through now — trained under
Cypher, measured against Cypher's standard, and eventually had to decide
what to do about it. He came out the other side with his own approach and
a clear opinion about what Cypher gets wrong. He is investing that opinion
in Vigil.

## Role

Secondary mentor to [[Wiki/PCs/Vigil]]. Former apprentice of [[Wiki/NPCs/Cypher]].
Pulls against Cypher's influence — where Cypher embodies Danger and
dismisses Superior, Meridian actively encourages Vigil to think, adapt,
and outmaneuver. His agenda is for Vigil to become something better than
the model Cypher represents.

## Party Relationship

Holds Influence over Vigil at start of play. His presence creates a
genuine mentor conflict: Vigil has two adults with authority over him
whose visions for his development are in direct tension.

## Influence

Holds Influence over [[Wiki/PCs/Vigil]] at start of play. Unlike Cypher's
permanent Protégé-rule hold, this Influence can shift normally through play.

## What the Team Knows

Cypher's former apprentice. Now operates independently. Believes Vigil
should surpass, not replicate, Cypher.

# Silver Savior

*First hero of the Silver Generation. Wielder of the Silver Spark.*

## Description

Covered in silvery metal. Capable of flight faster than anything previously seen. Nigh-invulnerable. As strong as Champion, if not stronger. Wielder of the Silver Spark and all its powers. Bright, shining, powerful — and a representative of an entirely new class of superhuman.

## Role

Widely acknowledged as the first Silver Generation hero. Fought threats of a cosmic scale rather than mere criminals.

Notable battles:
- **Doctor Infinity** — an all-powerful time-altering android
- **The Jabberwock** — a monster risen from fiction itself, seeking to convert all of reality into words on a page

Many of his battles were struggles over the fate of the entire world. His battles leveled city blocks, changed the color of the sky for days, left the whole city smelling of ozone and smoke.

## Current Status

The Silver Generation is still largely around and in power but reaching the end of its time. Silver Savior's exact current status is unknown — likely semi-retired or looking for a successor. A portion of the Silver Spark is now wielded by Silver Ace of the Modern Generation.

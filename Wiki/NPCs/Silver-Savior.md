# Silver Savior

**Generation:** [[Wiki/Lore/Silver-Generation]]  
**Status:** Unknown (likely retired or aged)

## Overview

The first widely acknowledged representative of the [[Wiki/Lore/Silver-Generation]] — the hero who defined what it meant for superpowers to level up. Most suprologists regard him as the opening figure of the Silver era.

## Powers

- Covered in silvery metal
- Flight faster than ever seen before
- Nigh-invulnerable
- Strength comparable to Champion
- Wielder of the **Silver Spark** and all its associated powers

## Notable Battles

Silver Savior fought foes on a wholly different scale than his predecessors:

- **Doctor Infinity** — an all-powerful, time-altering android
- **The Jabberwock** — a monster risen from fiction itself, threatening to convert all of reality into words on a page

His battles were often struggles over the fate of the entire world, not merely [[Wiki/World/Halcyon-City]].

## Legacy

The Silver Spark he wielded has persisted beyond him — a portion of it is now wielded by **Silver Ace**, an apprentice hero of the [[Wiki/Lore/Modern-Generation]]. Silver Savior's template — bright, shining, supremely powerful — defined the Silver Generation's aesthetic and self-image.

# Marcus Chen

## Description

Nineteen years old. Community college student who works night shifts at a bodega in Eastside. Tired-eyed, working-class. Shorted out half his block two nights ago — the first visible manifestation of his electrokinetic powers. Does not yet understand what is happening to him.

## Role

Pinnacle Solutions extraction target. Identified through the same predictive biomarker screening that flagged Elena Reyes. His manifestation has been active for two days — visible enough to register on Pinnacle's sensors and prompt an extraction team deployment.

Glitch is en route to reach him before Pinnacle does. Time is the most critical of the three simultaneous extractions.

# Subject 17

## Description

Unknown. Communicates only through voice-modulated audio transmissions bounced through multiple proxies. Voice is educated and precise; underneath the modulator, speech patterns suggest controlled fear. Refuses to reveal identity. May have powers — former Prometheus test subject suggests as much.

Real name unknown. Hero name (if any) unknown.

## Role

A Prometheus Labs survivor who has been watching Pinnacle Solutions and reached out to the team when they started pulling on the same thread. They possess detailed intelligence on Pinnacle's current sweep — names, locations, timetables — suggesting sustained covert surveillance or inside access. Their primary stated goal was to reach Cypher.

## Party Relationship

**[Session 1]** First and only contact: an audio-only encrypted transmission on Cypher's old secure channel. Provided the names and locations of three Pinnacle extraction targets (Marcus Chen, Deshawn Williams, Lily Vasquez). Refused to reveal identity. Closed with a message for Cypher: *"Tell him Subject 17 remembers what he did. And that the debt isn't settled yet."* The nature of whatever history exists between Subject 17 and Cypher is unknown to the team.

## Influence

No direct Influence over PCs established. [Session 1]

## What the Team Knows

- Identifies as "Subject 17" — a former Prometheus Labs test subject
- Claims Marcus Webb's people "took them, tested them, and tried to dispose of them" eight years ago
- Possesses current intelligence on Pinnacle's extraction sweep: target names, locations, timetables
- Transmitting from within Halcyon City (signal analysis: within a few miles of the base)
- Uses Cypher's old encryption protocols — either worked with Cypher years ago or obtained the protocols from someone who did
- Has unresolved history with Cypher: "the debt isn't settled yet"

## GM Truth

*(gm_only)* Identity, powers, and the nature of the Cypher connection are campaign reveals. Subject 17 is likely powered — the Prometheus program was designed to activate latent markers, not just identify them, which raises the question of what they did to subjects. Their intelligence on Pinnacle's sweep suggests active counter-surveillance. Their relationship to Cypher may explain why Cypher has been absent for three days.

## History

**[Session 1]** Made contact with the team via Cypher's old secure channel after detecting their investigation of Pinnacle Solutions. Provided actionable intel on three extraction targets. Declined to meet in person or reveal identity. Requested confirmation of Cypher's status and an opportunity to speak with him directly.

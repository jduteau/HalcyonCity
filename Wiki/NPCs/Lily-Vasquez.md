# Lily Vasquez

## Description

Fourteen years old. Resident of the Miller Street group home. Has not yet manifested visible powers — she was flagged by Pinnacle's predictive biomarker screening before any manifestation occurred. She does not know what she is or what is coming for her.

## Role

Pinnacle Solutions pre-manifestation extraction target. The most vulnerable of the three active targets. Pinnacle wants her for "observation and pre-manifestation study" — meaning they intend to take her before she even knows she has powers.

Cinder is en route to intercept.

## Party Relationship

**[Session 1]** Not yet encountered. Name and location provided by [[Wiki/NPCs/Subject-17]]. Subject 17's voice carried particular weight when naming her: *"She's fourteen. And they want to take her before she even knows what she is."* Cinder visibly reacted; Vigil warned him not to escalate.

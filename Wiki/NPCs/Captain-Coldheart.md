# Captain Coldheart

*Gold Generation villain. Mortal enemy of [[Wiki/NPCs/Flying-Freedom]].*

## Description

An evil genius with an aerial doomcraft. Classic Gold Generation villain: powerful, theatrical, and dangerous enough to threaten the entire city.

## Role

Captain Coldheart was the mortal enemy of [[Wiki/NPCs/Flying-Freedom]]. His attack on Halcyon City with his aerial doomcraft was the battle that killed Flying Freedom — even as she saved the city from him.

## Current Status

Gold Generation villains are either gone, in hiding, or trying to reclaim old relevance. Captain Coldheart's current status is unknown.

# Flying Freedom

**Real name:** Maggie MacIntyre  
**Generation:** [[Wiki/Lore/Gold-Generation]]  
**Status:** Deceased (died in the line of duty)

## Overview

The first publicly acknowledged superhero in [[Wiki/World/Halcyon-City]] — and in the world. Historians know that superhumans existed before her, scattered throughout history, but Flying Freedom was the first public figure to whom the term "superhero" could reasonably be applied.

## Appearance & Powers

- Pilot's jacket, cap, and goggles
- American flag emblazoned on her back
- Power of flight — newsreel footage of the era marveled at how she soared over Halcyon's tallest towers

## History

Maggie MacIntyre fought criminals and madmen with flair and heroism. She was not immortal or invulnerable.

She died saving Halcyon City from her mortal enemy, **Captain Coldheart**, and his aerial doomcraft. Her death did not end superheroism — it ignited it. Champion and the Haunt had already taken up her struggle, and Halcyon would never be the same.

## Legacy

Flying Freedom's example drew superhumans to Halcyon from across the country and beyond. She is the reason the city became the world's superhuman capital. Every subsequent generation of heroes stands on the foundation she built.

See [[Wiki/Lore/Superhero-Generations]] for the broader context of how she launched the Gold Generation era.

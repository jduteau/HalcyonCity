# Flying Freedom

*Maggie MacIntyre. First public superhero. Gold Generation.*

## Description

Cloaked in a pilot's jacket, cap, and goggles, with the American flag emblazoned on her back. She could fly — newsreel footage of the era marveled at how she soared over Halcyon's tallest towers.

## Role

Flying Freedom was the first public figure to whom the term "superhero" could reasonably be ascribed. She fought criminals and madmen with flair and heroism, inspiring the generation that followed her. She was not immortal and not invulnerable.

## Party Relationship

Historical figure — predates the campaign by roughly 80 years.

# Marcus Webb

## Description

Silver-haired, polished, the kind of face that reads as trustworthy on a corporate website. Veteran-turned-entrepreneur. His public profile is philanthropy, national security consulting, and veteran advocacy. Nothing in his public record touches the things he actually does.

## Role

CEO of [[Wiki/Factions/Pinnacle-Solutions]]. The operational mind behind a city-wide metahuman acquisition sweep and the institutional successor to Prometheus Labs. He is the face behind the Prometheus Conspiracy — the man who took research into "identification and activation of latent metahuman genetic markers" from a shuttered lab and weaponized it through a private military company.

## Party Relationship

**[Session 1]** Identified but not encountered in person. Vigil found his name and history in public records and Cypher's archives. The team knows he is the architect of tonight's operations.

## Influence

No direct Influence relationships with PCs. [Session 1]

## What the Team Knows

- CEO of Pinnacle Solutions
- Prior role: Project Director at Prometheus Labs, shut down eight years ago for allegations of unethical human experimentation
- Founded Pinnacle Solutions six months after Prometheus Labs closed
- Pinnacle has government contracts and is publicly presented as a security consultancy
- He is the person who ordered tonight's extraction sweep

## GM Truth

Webb ran Prometheus Labs' metahuman identification program and walked away when it was shut down — the records were sealed and subjects never identified. He brought the research methodology into Pinnacle Solutions under corporate cover. The city-wide biomarker screening (through infiltrated university and medical health systems) is his program. He is not just acquiring metahumans for study; the true scope of what Pinnacle does with acquired subjects is not yet revealed to the team.

Subject 17 is a survivor of his earlier Prometheus program and clearly holds a grievance specific enough to have personal knowledge of Cypher.

## History

**[Session 1]** Identified via public records and Cypher's archived intelligence. Connected to Prometheus Labs shut-down eight years ago. Pinnacle Solutions, which Webb founded immediately after, is now conducting extraction operations against newly-manifested metahumans throughout Halcyon City.

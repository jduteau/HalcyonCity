# Elena Reyes

## Description

Twenty-two years old. Physics PhD candidate at Halcyon City University, quantum field theory track. Dark hair, quick analytical eyes, the kind of person whose first response to an impossible situation is to start building a model of it. She carries exhaustion like a second skin — the grad-student kind, compounded now by trauma. When her powers are active, her eyes glow deep orange-gold and the air around her hands bends light in ways that shouldn't be possible.

She was wearing flannel pajamas when Pinnacle came for her. She has not yet had a chance to change.

## Role

Elena is a newly manifested nova — light and heat powers, currently uncontrolled — who was rescued by the team during their first operational mission. She has no family, no safe apartment, and no prior connection to the powered community. She is a physicist who now breaks every law she has ever studied. Her analytical mind makes her a potential intelligence asset; her uncontrolled powers make her a liability in the field until she develops control.

She is also still an active Pinnacle Solutions target.

## Party Relationship

**[Session 1]** Elena was the team's first rescue. She was hostile-but-not-hostile on arrival — terrified, not aggressive — and cooperated once Cinder established trust by showing his own fire. She listened when Vigil told her the truth about Prometheus Labs and Pinnacle, then asked every question that mattered. When offered a chance to walk away, she refused: her apartment is a crime scene, her records are compromised, she has no family, and she was hunted before she even knew what she was. She chose to stay. She is now stationed at base on comms as the team operates in the field.

## What the Team Knows

- Age 22, physics grad student at Halcyon City University (quantum field theory)
- Parents deceased — car accident, three years ago; no siblings; no close family
- Manifested uncontrolled light/heat powers the night Pinnacle operatives attacked her in her apartment
- Attended a university wellness screening approximately three weeks ago: bloodwork, nurse without a badge, white van watching her street afterward
- Powers are active but entirely uncontrolled; generates intense heat and light under stress
- Made the deduction herself: Pinnacle has a biomarker test for pre-manifestation metahumans

## GM Truth

Elena's powers are genuinely new — she is not concealing prior knowledge or ability. Her analytical intelligence makes her unusually quick at pattern-recognition for a traumatized civilian. The Prometheus biomarker screening correctly flagged her; this makes her a data point in a larger research program. How many others like her were flagged, taken, or never warned — she does not yet know.

Her stability at base is temporary. She is still a Pinnacle target, her powers are dangerous when she loses emotional regulation, and she has no support structure except this team of strangers.

## History

**[Session 1]** Found at the center of Warehouse 17, surrounded by six unconscious Pinnacle tactical operatives she had incapacitated during a power surge she could not control. Was talked down by Cinder, went critical from exhaustion, was flown out by Cinder and discharged a massive burst of energy over the waterfront. Recovered at Cypher's base. Disclosed the wellness screening. Listened to Vigil's briefing on Pinnacle Solutions and Prometheus Labs, then chose to remain with the team rather than find an exit.

Left alone at base on comms as the team deployed for three simultaneous extractions.

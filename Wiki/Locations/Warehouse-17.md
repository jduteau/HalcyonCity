# Warehouse 17

## Overview

Part of the Dock Street shipping complex — a block of former industrial buildings that has been "under redevelopment" for six years. Translation: abandoned, useful, and deliberately overlooked by anyone with a badge. Warehouse 17 is cavernous, filled with rusted shipping containers arranged in haphazard rows, with intermittent emergency lighting.

The surrounding district had been cleared of its usual population before the team arrived. The local homeless community had vanished.

## Description

A large, disused industrial warehouse with old emergency lighting that flickers red. Accessed from a loading bay facing the main lot. A service alley runs along one side with access to maintenance tunnels below. The dock district location means it smells of salt and diesel even indoors.

After Session 1: one section of the exterior brick wall has been melted through — edges still glowing orange in the aftermath of Elena Reyes' uncontrolled discharge. A shipping container inside was buckled and folded by the heat. The concrete floor at the center point of the incident is fused where it began glowing cherry-red. Six unconscious Pinnacle Solutions operatives were left at the scene.

## Current Status

**[Session 1]** Crime scene. Pinnacle extraction team (six operatives) was incapacitated here by Elena Reyes' power surge. The team extracted Elena through the service alley and maintenance tunnels before emergency services arrived. Emergency services (fire, police) likely responded to the energy discharge over the waterfront — the scene has probably been discovered.

Pinnacle will know this operation failed. They will retrieve or account for their operatives. What they report or suppress to local law enforcement is unknown.

## Known Inhabitants

None — abandoned industrial site. Pinnacle used it as a staging point for the Elena Reyes extraction.

## Team History

**[Session 1]** First operational scene. The team entered via maintenance tunnels and service ladder, discovered Elena Reyes in the middle of a critical power surge, talked her down (partially), and extracted under fire-emergency conditions when she discharged. Departed before emergency services arrived.

## GM Notes

*(gm_only)* The six operatives left behind are a thread — alive, presumably, but incapacitated and at a crime scene. Pinnacle will either recover them quietly or let them take the fall. If emergency services found them, there may be a public record of powered activity at Dock Street. The melted wall and deformed shipping container are not explainable by conventional means.

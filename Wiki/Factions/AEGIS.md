# A.E.G.I.S.

**Advanced Expert Group for Intervention and Security**

The primary governmental agency for the metahuman world. Headquartered in [[Wiki/World/Halcyon-City]].

## Overview

A.E.G.I.S. roots go back to the [[Wiki/Lore/Gold-Generation]], but it has changed substantially over the decades. Today it is a full-spectrum metahuman law enforcement and intelligence organization.

## Capabilities

- Agents and strike teams
- Metahuman holding facilities
- Extensive intelligence network
- Recruitment and cultivation of new metahuman talent

## Role

When metahuman law enforcement is needed, it comes from A.E.G.I.S. This makes them simultaneously:
- A constant thorn in the side of Halcyon's superheroes
- One of their strongest allies

## Recruitment

A.E.G.I.S. keeps files on everyone. It actively fosters and recruits new talent — including the young. It's always on the lookout for those who can do what needs to be done to keep the world safe from genuine superhuman threats.

**Important caveat**: Joining A.E.G.I.S. is not about being a superhero. It's about being an agent — a servant of a greater purpose, required to make hard decisions. It can change you, and not always for the better.

## Relationship to Heroes

A.E.G.I.S. straddles the line between ally and antagonist for most superhero teams. They don't always agree with heroes on methods or priorities, but they're indispensable for handling threats that require institutional backing.

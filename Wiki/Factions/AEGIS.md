# A.E.G.I.S.

*Advanced Expert Group for Intervention and Security.*

## Overview

A.E.G.I.S. is the primary governmental agency for the metahuman world. Its roots go back to the Gold Generation, but it has changed dramatically over the decades. It now has agents and strike teams, metahuman holding facilities, a tremendous intelligence network, and more.

When metahuman law enforcement is necessary, it comes from A.E.G.I.S. — making it somewhere between a constant thorn in the side of Halcyon's superheroes and one of their strongest allies.

Headquartered in [[Wiki/World/Halcyon-City]].

## Goals

**Stated:** Keep the world safe from genuine superhuman threats.

**Operational reality:** A.E.G.I.S. keeps files on everyone. It fosters and recruits new talent wherever possible — including among the young. Joining A.E.G.I.S. means being an agent, a servant of a greater purpose that pushes you to make hard decisions. It can change you, and not always for the better.

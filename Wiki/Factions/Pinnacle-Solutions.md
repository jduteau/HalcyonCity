# Pinnacle Solutions

## Overview

Pinnacle Solutions is a private military and security contractor operating out of Halcyon City with government contracts and a clean public record. In practice, it is the operational arm of an ongoing covert metahuman acquisition program — the direct institutional successor to [[Wiki/Conspiracy/Prometheus-Conspiracy]]. It runs extraction teams against newly-manifested metahumans identified through its proprietary biomarker screening protocol, which has been infiltrated into university and medical health systems throughout the city.

Founded eight years ago by CEO [[Wiki/NPCs/Marcus-Webb]], six months after Prometheus Labs — the research division he previously ran — was shut down for unethical human experimentation.

## Leadership

- **CEO**: [[Wiki/NPCs/Marcus-Webb]] — veteran-turned-entrepreneur; founder; architect of the metahuman acquisition program

## Goals

**Stated:** Private security consulting, government and corporate contracts, veteran services.

**True (gm_only):** Systematic identification, extraction, and acquisition of metahumans — before and after manifestation. The research methodology developed at Prometheus Labs has been operationalized into a city-wide surveillance and interdiction apparatus. What Pinnacle does with acquired subjects is not yet established in play.

## Team Relationship

**[Session 1]** The team identified Pinnacle Solutions as the organization behind the Warehouse 17 extraction attempt against [[Wiki/NPCs/Elena-Reyes]] and the three active sweeps targeting [[Wiki/NPCs/Marcus-Chen]], [[Wiki/NPCs/Deshawn-Williams]], and [[Wiki/NPCs/Lily-Vasquez]]. Glitch's failed intrusion attempt tripped Pinnacle's security ICE — Pinnacle now knows an outside party is investigating them.

## GM Notes

*(gm_only)* Pinnacle has military-grade digital security (ICE that detected Glitch's intrusion). Their extraction teams are tactically proficient — Elena incapacitated six operatives with uncontrolled powers and they still sent three vans. They have government off-book contracts. Marcus Webb's broader agenda — what he actually does with acquired metahumans — is the next layer of the conspiracy to reveal.

The accelerated timeline (triggered by Glitch's intrusion) means Pinnacle is now operating at higher operational tempo across the city. They will notice when extraction teams fail to report in.

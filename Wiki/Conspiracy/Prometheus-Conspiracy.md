# Prometheus Conspiracy

## The Scheme

Eight years ago, Prometheus Labs — a research division run by [[Wiki/NPCs/Marcus-Webb]] — was developing a method to identify and activate latent metahuman genetic markers: a biomarker test capable of flagging individuals who would manifest powers *before* they manifested. The lab was shut down under allegations of unethical human experimentation. Records were sealed. Subjects were never publicly identified.

Webb was never charged. Six months later he founded [[Wiki/Factions/Pinnacle-Solutions]].

Pinnacle Solutions has taken the Prometheus methodology and scaled it. University health centers and medical systems throughout Halcyon City have been infiltrated; the biomarker screening is now running through routine wellness appointments. When a pre-manifestation individual is flagged, Pinnacle tracks them — white van surveillance, monitoring — and deploys extraction teams at or shortly after their first manifestation event.

The name "Prometheus" — the titan who gave fire to humanity — appears to be deliberate. Someone at Prometheus Labs or Pinnacle Solutions knows exactly what they are taking.

## Known Evidence

**[Session 1]**

- [[Wiki/NPCs/Elena-Reyes]] attended a university wellness screening three weeks before her manifestation: bloodwork, a nurse without a badge, a white van watching her street afterward
- Three unmarked Pinnacle vans were sent to extract her the night she manifested — before she had told anyone
- Vigil found a leaked internal Prometheus Labs memo in Cypher's archives describing the research focus: *"identification and activation of latent metahuman genetic markers"*
- Marcus Webb ran Prometheus Labs before it was shut down; founded Pinnacle Solutions immediately after
- [[Wiki/NPCs/Subject-17]] — a self-identified Prometheus test subject who survived whatever Webb's people did — confirmed the continuity between Prometheus and Pinnacle
- Pinnacle is running simultaneous extraction sweeps against at least three additional targets tonight: [[Wiki/NPCs/Marcus-Chen]], [[Wiki/NPCs/Deshawn-Williams]], [[Wiki/NPCs/Lily-Vasquez]]
- Lily Vasquez is a *pre-manifestation* target — Pinnacle is willing to acquire subjects before powers appear

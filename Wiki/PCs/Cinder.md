## Description

Sam is a teenager who caught fire — literally — somewhere between his
freshman and sophomore year, and has spent the time since trying not to
do it again by accident. He presents a cheerful, easy-going front that
most people buy completely. The front is not entirely fake; Sam genuinely
likes people, genuinely wants to help, and genuinely enjoys the work when
it goes well. What the front hides is the low-level dread that lives
under everything: the awareness that his powers are vast, imprecise, and
capable of hurting the people he's trying to protect.

In costume he burns orange-red at the edges when charged up, heat shimmer
distorting the air around him. Out of costume he's the kind of person
who always seems to be running a degree or two warmer than everyone else
in the room.

## Background

Sam's powers emerged without warning, without a mentor, and without an
instruction manual. Everything he knows about control he taught himself,
mostly through trial and error, mostly by learning what happens when he
gets it wrong. He's gotten it wrong enough times that the errors have
faces now. One of them belongs to Glitch.

He hangs out with Vigil constantly — partly because Vigil is the person
on this team who seems to have a plan for everything, and partly because
being around someone that controlled makes Sam feel slightly less like
a lit fuse. He'd never say any of this out loud.

## Special Features

**Burn**
Sam charges up by rolling +conditions currently marked.
- 10+: hold 3 Burn
- 7-9: hold 3 Burn, mark a condition
- Miss: hold 2 Burn and mark THREE conditions

All unspent Burn clears at end of scene.

Current Burn: 0 *(reset each scene; update mid-session as needed)*

**Flares** (chosen four):

- **Reality storm** — Spend 1 Burn to directly engage a threat using
  powers, rolling +Freak instead of +Danger. Causes unwanted collateral
  unless a second Burn is spent.
- **Shielding** — Spend 1 Burn to defend someone else from an immediate
  threat, rolling +Freak instead of +Savior.
- **Moat** — Spend 1 Burn to create a barrier of fire that holds back
  threats while Sam keeps attention on it. GM may call for more Burn
  against powerful enemies.
- **Overcharge** — Spend 2 Burn to automatically take a 10+ when
  unleashing powers.

**The reinforcing loop:**
More conditions → more Burn on charge-up → more flare use → more risk
of conditions. Track this carefully. Sam's Freak (+2) is the Label most
flares key off; his low Mundane (-1) means the human cost of his power
is always close to the surface.

**The wound:**
Sam once hurt Glitch when he lost control. This is not resolved. It lives
in how he moves around her, the half-second of extra care he takes when
she's nearby. It is the thing the triumphant celebration question —
*any fear in their eyes?* — is most likely to surface with her.

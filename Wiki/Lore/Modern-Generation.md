# Modern Generation

*Heroes born from the 1990s to today.* The newest generation — most still quite young. They don't have an agreed-upon name yet ("Modern," "New," "Young Generation" are all used interchangeably).

This is the generation the PCs belong to. See [[Wiki/PCs/_index]] for the player characters.

## Context

They grew up in a world where superhumans are normal. Dinosaurs on Main Street is just a thing that happens. Alien invasions are tourist attractions. They have a stronger innate grasp of superhuman issues than any prior generation — though that familiarity cuts both ways.

## Character

Unlike prior generations, the Modern Generation has full visibility into the entire superheroic record:
- The [[Wiki/Lore/Gold-Generation]]'s nobility and its easy blind spots
- The [[Wiki/Lore/Silver-Generation]]'s power and its domineering attitudes
- The [[Wiki/Lore/Bronze-Generation]]'s frustration and its ultimate failure to change the system

That history weighs on them. They know what worked and what failed. They're more aware of fame, celebrity, the internet, and the voices of everyone around them than any prior generation.

## Notable Figures

- **Cygnus**: Teen pop-star turned villain — emblematic of the generation's relationship with celebrity
- **Silver Ace**: Apprentice hero, wielder of a portion of the Silver Spark; heir to Silver Generation legacy

## Identity Questions They Face

- Do they change the world, or go for fame instead?
- Do they fix the system from within, or declare it broken?
- Do they follow their mentors, or define something entirely new?

The world watches with trepidation and excitement.

## Today

No single identity has crystallized yet. The Modern Generation is in the middle of its ascendance. [[Wiki/World/Halcyon-City]] doesn't yet know what it will become as they rise.

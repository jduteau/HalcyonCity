# Silver Generation

Heroes born from the 1950s to the 1960s. A sea change in Halcyon's superheroic scene: superpowers became dramatically more powerful, and with them came ever-greater threats.

## Overview

[[Wiki/NPCs/Silver-Savior]] is considered the first widely acknowledged Silver Generation hero — silvery metal, nigh-invulnerable, faster than anything seen before, as strong as Champion, wielder of the Silver Spark. A new kind of superhuman.

Silver Savior fought Doctor Infinity (a time-altering android) and the Jabberwock (a monster risen from fiction itself, intent on converting all reality into words on a page). The battles of this generation were often struggles over the fate of the entire world — leveling whole city blocks, changing the color of the sky for days.

## Super Teams

Such incredible threats drove heroes to band together. The Exemplars, the Silver Saviors, the Amazing Eight — all originated in this generation. Villains formed their own alliances in response.

## Social Context

The Silver Generation made great early strides on mundane struggles, pushing for equal rights. But over time, as their power grew, their focus shifted ever upward to greater and greater threats — largely abandoning social issues and conflicts with ordinary criminals or corrupt politicians.

## Current Status

Still largely around and in power today, but reaching the end of their time. Many are looking toward retirement and would love to find successors they can shape directly into new versions of themselves — ensuring things continue exactly as they have.

## Relevance to Campaign

The Silver Generation dominates the current power structure. Their domineering attitudes and desire to control their own succession are a direct source of tension for Modern Generation PCs.

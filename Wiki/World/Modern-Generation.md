# Modern Generation

Heroes born from the 1990s to today. The newest generation — still ascending, still unnamed.

## Overview

The Modern Generation lives in a world fully accustomed to superhumans. Dinosaurs on Main Street, alien invasions, escaped products of mad science — treated as somewhere between average city problems and tourist attractions. This generation has a stronger, more innate grasp of superhuman issues than any prior generation.

They don't have a consensus name yet. "Modern Generation" is a placeholder used alongside "The New Generation," "The Young Generation," and others. They haven't yet made their mark, nor developed as strong an identity as those before them.

## Notable Figures (World Canon)

- **Cygnus** — teen pop-star turned villain; one of the splashier Modern Generation figures
- **Silver Ace** — apprenticed to older heroes; wielder of a portion of the Silver Spark originally held by [[Wiki/NPCs/Silver-Savior]]

## Character

The Modern Generation has proved itself simultaneously more hopeful and more skeptical than any prior generation. They see clearly:
- The Gold Generation's nobility, and its all-too-easy oversight
- The Silver Generation's power and success, and its domineering attitudes
- The Bronze Generation's frustration and search for new alternatives, and its ultimate failure to change anything significantly

All of that history weighs on them. They're still deciding who they want to be and what world they want to make. They're also more aware of fame, the internet, and the voices of everyone around them than any prior generation.

## Relevance to Campaign

The PCs are the Modern Generation. This is their moment, still unwritten.
